RECOMMENDED IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS:
================================
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Size: 80x80 to 120x120 pixels (square format preferred)
- Style: Clean, professional logos
- Background: Transparent or white
- Color: Official brand colors

QUICK DOWNLOAD SOURCES:
======================
1. Simple Icons (https://simpleicons.org/) - Free SVG icons for all major technologies
2. DevIcons (https://devicon.dev/) - Developer-focused icons
3. Iconify (https://iconify.design/) - Large collection of icons
4. Official brand websites - Most reliable source for official logos

EASY METHOD:
============
Visit https://simpleicons.org/ and search for each technology name. Download the SVG and convert to PNG, or use the PNG versions if available.

Note: Make sure to respect copyright and use official logos or properly licensed icons.
